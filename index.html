about carsilon

CARSILON is a commuter-based carsharing platform, in which community members share their private vehicles

Advantages for passengers

    Easy to use

    Find a car 1 click.

  • Easy To Join a Community

    Public communities are available for all users
    Private communities require invitation

  • Inter-community communication

    One user can belong to several communities that extends the variety of ride options.

Advantages for drivers

  • Easy to use

    Find a fellow traveller in 1 click.

  • You drive today!

    Drivers can share rides and drive less.

  • Get Benefits

    Communities can encourage most active drivers.

Advantages for community

  • Empower your Community

    Engage community members to communicate more.

  • Easy to configure the system

    User and community management via Site Admin Module.

  • Inter-community communication

    One user can belong to several communities that extends the variety of ride options.

  • Public and Private Communities

    Public communities are accessible and can be joined by any member in your Slack team.

    Private communities can only be accessed by people specifically invited to the channel.

  • Easy to integrate

    CARsilon can be easily integrated with any payment system.
